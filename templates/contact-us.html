About Us

RENEGADES WELDING, LLC. is a full service Welding, Fabrication & Blacksmith Shop.

Renegades Welding LLC is owned & operated by Alejandro Ortiz. After more than a decade of working in the welding industry Alejandro decided to use his experience and perfected skills to established Renegades Welding llc in 2015. Since then he has worked in a variety of projects and has built a loyal clientele base.

Renegades Welding can save you and your business money by repairing rather than replacing equipment. we always strived to provide quality workmanship at a fair price. Feel free to contact us for all your welding needs.
